Proposed New Routes

Route A: Lincoln City FC Shuttle
Route: Your Local Area → Sincil Bank Stadium
Departure: You will be emailed of the Departure Time 1 day before the match
Operating Days: Home match days only
Journey Time: Approximately 30 minutes (arrive 1 hour before kick-off)
Capacity: 1 minibus with 16-18 seats

Key Benefits:

  • Skip match day traffic and parking hassles
  • Direct drop-off at Sincil Bank Stadium
  • Return service after final whistle
  • Support the Imps in comfort and style
Route B: Summer Cromer Day Trip
Route: Your Local Area → Cromer → Return
Frequency: Daily departures during summer season
Operating Days: June to September
Journey Time: Full day trip (8 hours including stops)
Capacity: 1 minibus with 16-18 seats

Key Benefits:

  • Explore beautiful Norfolk coastal towns
  • No driving stress or parking fees
  • More time to explore Cromer's attractions
  • Enjoy Cromer's famous crab and sandy beaches
Route C: Nottingham Christmas Shuttle
Route: Your Local Area → Old Market Square
Schedule: 14th of November every year
Operating Days: Annual Christmas market trip
Journey Time: Approximately 45 minutes
Capacity: 1 minibus with 16-18 seats

Key Benefits:

  • Skip traffic and parking hassles in Nottingham
  • Direct drop-off at Old Market Square
  • Return service after Christmas market visit
  • Enjoy festive atmosphere without driving stress
Route D: Christmas Hamleys Shuttle
Route: Your Local Area → Hamleys London
Schedule: Once a year around Christmas - Pick up at 7AM Drop off at 10AM, pick up at 5PM and Drop Off at 8PM
Operating Days: Annual Christmas shopping trip
Journey Time: Approximately 2 hours 45 minutes
Capacity: 1 minibus with 16-18 seats

Key Benefits:

  • Skip London traffic and expensive parking
  • Direct drop-off at world-famous Hamleys toy store
  • Full day to explore London's Christmas attractions
  • Perfect for Christmas shopping without driving stress
